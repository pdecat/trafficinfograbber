TrafficInfoGrabber (TIG) is a simple traffic information grabber for Paris, France.
Information gathered from Sytadin.fr and InfoTrafic.com.

Go to "Menu -> Preferences" to customize the application to your taste (quit/refresh/etc. buttons, notifications, etc.).

FEATURES:
- Optimized Traffic view for mobile phones
- Car Mode events handling
- Third party app optional button
- Long press quit button to kill the app's process
- Free software (GPL v3+)
- and lots more (check the preferences)

Feel free to send suggestions by email (pdecat+tig@gmail.com).
